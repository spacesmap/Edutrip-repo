ABOUT EduTrip

Edutrip ( EduTrip International Group ), a combination of education and travel cross-border combination of training team and travel companies."EduTrip Hong Kong", "WeIntern Micro Internship", "EduTrip • Hawaii"( WeIntern micro-practice) and "CNewTravel Travel" and other projects. Has successfully held too many Hong Kong winter project, the participants from all over the country colleges and universities, the number of nearly 1,000 people.EduTrip works with the world's top 500 companies to provide students with quality learning resources and effective training results. Successful completion of the "Winter Camp" project will be awarded by EduTrip to the world's top 500 internship certificates and letters of recommendation, as well as internationally recognized volunteer certificates, to enrich the resume of students who are studying abroad and studying in practice.
